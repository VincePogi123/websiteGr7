Hair Conditioner

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

Apply to wet hair and massage throughly to every part of scalp until all is covered and smooth then rinse after 3 minutes.

Indgredients

aqua (water), stearyl alcohol, cetyl alcohol, Cocos nucifera (coconut oil), Helianthus annuus (sunflower) seed oil , Glycine soja (soybean) oil, cocoglycerides, Aloe barbadensis (aloe vera) leaf juice, Persea gratissima (avocado) oil, olus oil, diheptyl succinate, capryloyl glycerin/sebacic acid copolymer, p-anisic acid, levulinic acid, sodium levulinate, glycerin, fragrance, citric acid, myristyl alcohol, 1-eicosanol, sorbic acid, hydroxypropyltrimonium honey, cetrimonium chloride, potassium sorbate, sodium benzoate. (Exact ingredients may vary by product version; always check the label.)

Precaution

If in eyes, hold eyelids apart and flush the eye continuously with running water. If skin or hair contact occurs, remove contaminated clothing and flush skin and hair with running water. If inhaled, remove from contaminated area. Apply artificial respiration if not breathing. Ingestion.
