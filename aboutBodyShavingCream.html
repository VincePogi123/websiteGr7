Body Shaving Cream

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

Wet your face with warm water to soften hair. Apply a small amount of shaving cream to your hands or directly on your face. Lather it up, covering the areas to be shaved. Shave as usual, then rinse your face with water.

Indgredients

Aqua (Water) Glyceri Stearic, Acid Sodium Hydroxide Fragrance, Various moisturizing agents and preservatives(Ingredient lists may vary; always check the label.)

Precaution

Avoid contact with eyes. Conduct a skin test first. Stop using and consult a doctor if skin becomes red, swollen and itchy during use. Keep out of reach of children.
