Body Moisturizer

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

Cleanse your face thoroughly. Take a small amount of the moisturizer. Apply evenly on your face and neck. Gently massage in until fully absorbed. Use daily for best results, ideally in the morning and evening.

Indgredients

Aqua (Water) Glycerin Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) Licorice Extract Sunscreen agents (varies by product) Various emollients and preservatives Fragrance (Ingredient lists may vary; always check the product label for specifics.)

Precaution

For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Conduct a patch test before use, especially if you have sensitive skin. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Keep out of reach of children and store in a cool, dry place.
