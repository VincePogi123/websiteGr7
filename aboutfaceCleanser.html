Cleanser

Usage

Age Restrictions: For all ages

apply to the wet or dry skins, put in to your face and massage gently, and rinse off.

Indgredients

Aqua, Glycerin, Cetearyl Alcohol, Panthenol, Niacinamide, Pantolactone, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Sodium Benzoate, Citric Acid
