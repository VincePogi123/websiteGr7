Sunscreen

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

Apply a generous amount on face and body. Put sunscreen after moisturizer or before make up. Reapply throughout the day for better protection from UVA and UVB rays, especially outdoors.

Indgredients

Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate, Water, 1,3-Butylene glycol, polymethylsilsesquioxane, Dicaprylyl Carbonate, Stearalkonium Hectorite, Propylene Carbonate, Sclerotium Gum, Sodium Polyacrylate, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice Powder, Glycerin, Panthenol, Escin, Ruscus Aculeatus Root Extract, Ammonium Glycyrrhizate, Centella Asiatica Extract, Hydrolyzed Yeast Protein, Calendula Officinalis Flower Extract, Fragrance, Phenoxyethanol, Chlorphenesin.

Precaution

Avoid contact with eyes. Conduct a skin test first. Stop using and consult a doctor if skin becomes red, swollen and itchy during use. Keep out of reach of children.
