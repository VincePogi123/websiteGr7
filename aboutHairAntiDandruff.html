Hair Anti-Dandruff

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

apply gently to the scalp with circular motion for 2 minutes and rinse it off thoroughly without leaving anything behind.

Indgredients

Pyrithione Zinc (1%), cocamidopropyl betaine, Water, sodiumxylenesulfonate, sodiumlauryl sulfate, dimethicon, sodium laureth sulfate, sodium benzoate, glycol distearate, guar, hydroxypropyltrimoniumchloride, sodium chloride, magnesiumcarbonate hydroxide, zinc carbonate (Exact ingredients may vary by product version; always check the label.)

Precaution

If in eyes, hold eyelids apart and flush the eye continuously with running water. If skin or hair contact occurs, remove contaminated clothing and flush skin and hair with running water. If inhaled, remove from contaminated area. Apply artificial respiration if not breathing. Ingestion.
