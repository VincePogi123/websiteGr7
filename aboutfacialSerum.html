Serum

Usage

Age Restrictions: for teens and above only

Apply a thin layer to clean face after tone and before heavier creams. Gently tap your face until product has been fully absorbed.

Indgredients

Water, Niacinamide, Glycerin, Dipropylene Glycol, Butylene Glycol, 1,2-Hexanediol, Hydroxyethyl Urea, Glycereth-25 PCA Isostearate, Tranexamic Acid, Zinc PCA, Hippophae Rhamnoides Fruit Extract, Allantoin, Sodium Hyaluronate, Glycosyl Trehalose, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Panthenol, Hydrogenated Starch Hydrolysate, Sodium Polyacrylate, Betaine, Tromethamine, Phenyl Trimethicone, Polyglyceryl-10 Oleate, Ethylhexylglycerin, Hydrogenated Lecithin, Disodium EDTA, Tocopherol.

Precaution

Avoid contact with eyes. Conduct a skin test first. Stop using and consult a doctor if skin becomes red, swollen and itchy during use. Keep out of reach of children.
