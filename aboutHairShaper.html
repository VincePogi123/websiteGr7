Hair Shaper

Usage

Age Restrictions: teens and above

Place the product at the root of your hairs and rub it a little until it hardens and able to style your hair in any way you want.

Indgredients

kaolin, Ricinus communis (castor) seed oil, Cocos nucifera (coconut) oil, hydrogenated castor oil, glyceryl caprylate, Glycine soja (soybean) oil, p-anisic acid, tocopherol (Vitamin E) (Exact ingredients may vary by product version; always check the label.)

Precaution

The proper amount of wax: Avoid applying too much wax to your hair. Otherwise, your hair may end up looking too greasy. About a pea-sized amount of wax is enough for short to medium-length hair. Layered styles: Apply the wax to your hair ends only by twirling the tips between your fingers.
