Hair Shampoo

Usage

Age Restrictions: 15+

Wet your body with water. Apply to scalp and hair and massage smoothly for 3-5 minutes before rinsing it off with water.

Indgredients

Water, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Perfume, Sodium Benzoate, Dimethiconol, Citric Acid, Carbomer, Menthol, PPG-9, Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride, TEA-Dodecylbenzenesulfonate, Disodium EDTA, Trideceth-10, Caffeine, Cyclotetrasiloxane, Phenoxyethanol, Propylene Glycol, Mentha Piperita (Peppermint) Leaf Extract, Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate, Lactic Acid, Glucose, Potassium Sorbate.(Exact ingredients may vary by product version; always check the label.)

Precaution

If in eyes, hold eyelids apart and flush the eye continuously with running water. If skin or hair contact occurs, remove contaminated clothing and flush skin and hair with running water. If inhaled, remove from contaminated area. Apply artificial respiration if not breathing. Ingestion.
